Gameplay & Rules

How do I create a custom room? expand_more
To create a custom room, click on the "Create Custom Room" button in the Lobby header. You can then select the game type, set a password for private games, and configure time limits. Once created, share the room code with your friends.
What happens if my opponent disconnects? expand_more
If an opponent disconnects during a match, they have a 2-minute grace period to reconnect. If they fail to return within that time, you will automatically win the match by forfeiture and receive the corresponding ranking points.
Are the games turn-based or real-time? expand_more
Most of our board games are turn-based but played in real-time sessions. This means you must make your move within a specific timer duration (usually 30 to 60 seconds per turn). Games like "Pixel Dots" require quick thinking!

Account & Profile

How can I change my avatar? expand_more
Navigate to your profile by clicking your avatar in the top right corner. Select "Edit Profile" and choose from our library of pixel art avatars or upload your own.
I forgot my password. How do I reset it? expand_more
On the login screen, click "Forgot Password?". Enter your registered email address, and we will send you a secure link to reset your password.

Technical Issues

The game is lagging. What should I do? expand_more
Please ensure you have a stable internet connection. We recommend using Chrome or Firefox for the best performance. If issues persist, try clearing your browser cache or disabling heavy extensions.
